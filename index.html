The story behind the name.

While searching for a name. The creator of the restaurant said I want something that was completely off base. Something that really doesn't relate to each other but stood out. Thus landing upon Chum & Blow. While it may seem off color, it really doesn't mean anything. Just two words that seem silly together. But, our mission is to give you the best experience you've had in a while. Trust us, it's fun!
